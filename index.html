Our Services

NEURO-PSYCHIATRIST

Neuropsychiatry combines neurology and psychiatry to understand and treat mental disorders related to neurological conditions.

GENERAL PHYSICIAN

General physicians offer primary medical care, diagnosing and treating common illnesses and conditions, serving as the initial point of contact for patients.

GASTROENTEROLOGIST

Gastroenterologists specialize in diagnosing and treating digestive system disorders, including conditions like ulcers, reflux, and irritable bowel syndrome.

PSYCHIATRIC

Psychiatrists are medical doctors who specialize in diagnosing and treating mental illnesses, often prescribing medication and therapy for conditions like depression and anxiety.

ORTHOPEDIC

Orthopedics focuses on treating musculoskeletal injuries and conditions, including fractures, joint problems, and sports injuries, often through surgery or rehabilitation.

GYNECOLOGIST

Gynecologists specialize in women's reproductive health, providing care for issues like pregnancy, menstruation, fertility, and menopause, including screenings and treatments.
